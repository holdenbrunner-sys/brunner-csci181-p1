About Fort Courage

Fort Courage Child Care Center, Inc. is a well established center dedicated to providing services that are in the best interest of the child. The center shall support and nurture physical, social, emotional, cognitive and creative growth of all children in a safe, caring, and pleasant environment.


Each day, Fort Courage provides a balanced approach to learning, giving you the peace of mind that your child will have fun as they develop socially, emotionally, physically, and intellectually. We enjoy the company of all the little ones from 6 weeks to 6 years.

Education Provided By Experts

    Highly Trained Staff

    Our staff are specially trained and have many years of experience in early childhoode education. They are dedicated to providing your child with the best education avalible.

    STARS to Quality Program

    We are currently a STARS to Quality Level 2 Facility. STARS programs are continuously improving their ability to provide a healthy, safe, and enriching environment for children. Programs have voluntarily enrolled in STARS to Quality to support Montana's children and families.

    Child & Adult Care Food Program (CACFP) Participant

    The Child and Adult Care Food Program (CACFP) is a federally funded program of the Food and Nutrition Services (FNS) of the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA). To learn more about the program you can visit Child and Adult Care Food Program (CACFP)
